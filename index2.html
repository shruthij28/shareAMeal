About The Program

This is a program that was founded to connect people during the holidays. Many people find themselves alone during this time of the year, and we want to change that. Through this website, anyone who wants to can search for a family to join during the holidays for a home-cooked meal. The same way, those who want to host a person during the holidays can find someone looking for a meal. With the use of this website, everyone can have a love-filled holiday.
